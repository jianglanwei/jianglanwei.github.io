Jianglan Wei

I am a senior undergrad in Artificial Intelligence at Huazhong University of Science and Technology and a research intern at UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka. My research focuses on robot learning, where I develop reinforcement learning algorithms to generate control policies and world models to anticipate the consequences of robot actions.
